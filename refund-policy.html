Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At 12thFailJobs, we strive to provide the best job platform experience for both workers and recruiters. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for our paid services.

Important Note: Basic job search and application services for workers are completely free. Refunds only apply to premium features and recruiter subscription plans.

2. Refund Eligibility

Recruiter Subscription Plans

Service Refund Period Conditions Status
Monthly Subscription 7 days No job postings created Full Refund
Quarterly Subscription 7 days No job postings created Full Refund
Annual Subscription 14 days No job postings created Full Refund
Featured Job Posts 24 hours Job not yet published Full Refund
Premium Job Posts 48 hours Less than 50% of subscription period used Partial Refund

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Used Services: Any subscription period that has been utilized
  • Published Job Posts: Jobs that have been live on the platform
  • Database Access: Worker database access fees
  • Background Verification: Third-party verification services
  • Processing Fees: Payment gateway and transaction fees
  • Custom Solutions: Tailored recruitment services
Exception: If our platform experiences technical issues that prevent you from using paid features for more than 48 hours, we may offer pro-rated refunds for the downtime period.

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact our support team at refunds@12failjob.com
  2. Provide your registered email and subscription details
  3. Explain the reason for the refund request
  4. Include any relevant screenshots or evidence
  5. Wait for our response within 3-5 business days

Refund Timeline

  • Request Processing: 3-5 business days
  • Approval: 1-2 business days after review
  • Refund Processing: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days

5. Valid Refund Reasons

Automatic Approval Reasons

  • Technical platform issues preventing service use
  • Accidental duplicate purchases
  • Services not delivered as described
  • Request made within the eligible refund period

Case-by-Case Review

  • Account access issues (if verified)
  • Platform downtime exceeding 48 hours
  • Billing errors or overcharges
  • Service quality concerns

Non-Valid Reasons

  • Changed mind after using the service
  • Found cheaper alternatives
  • Business closure or strategy change
  • Not finding suitable candidates

6. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, we may offer partial refunds:

Pro-Rated Refunds

  • For annual subscriptions with minimal usage
  • When technical issues affect only part of the service
  • For multiple job posting packages with unused credits

Calculation Method

Partial refunds are calculated based on:

  • Unused portion of subscription period
  • Number of unused job posting credits
  • Extent of service disruption
  • Minus any processing fees (2-3%)

7. Refund Methods

Original Payment Method

Refunds are typically processed back to the original payment method used:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the same card
  • UPI/Wallets: Refunded to the same UPI ID or wallet
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the same bank account

Alternative Methods

If the original payment method is unavailable:

  • Credit to your 12thFailJobs account wallet
  • Bank transfer to verified account
  • Store credit for future use (with 10% bonus)

8. Special Circumstances

Platform-Wide Issues

If our platform experiences significant technical issues:

  • Automatic extension of subscription periods
  • Pro-rated refunds for affected users
  • Priority support for affected accounts

Business Closure

In case of permanent business closure:

  • Pro-rated refunds for unused subscription periods
  • Data export assistance for affected users
  • 30-day notice period before service termination

9. Dispute Resolution

First-Level Resolution

If your refund request is denied, you can:

  • Request a review by our senior support team
  • Provide additional evidence or documentation
  • Escalate to our customer success manager

Formal Dispute Process

  • Submit a written dispute to disputes@12failjob.com
  • Include all relevant documentation and correspondence
  • Response within 10 business days
  • Option for mediation if unresolved

10. Refund Policy Updates

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on our website with effective date
  • Communicated via email to active subscribers
  • Applied prospectively (not retroactively)

Continued use of our services after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Our Commitment

We are committed to fair and transparent refund practices. If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, our support team is here to help.
